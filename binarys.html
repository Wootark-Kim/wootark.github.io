Why Binary?

Sometimes I have to remind myself the most basic of questions from time to time. One of which is:

Why do computers function off of a binary system?

This is because of the hardware design of transistors. Transistors can either conduct electricity (on) or deny it (off). This is represented as 1 or 0 (hence the binary system). Now throw is billions of transistors to represent multiple, complex scenarios and we essentially have a computer.
